üí° Pro-Tips for Managing Gemini

  • Be specific about aesthetics: Instead of "make it look good", direct them: "Use dark frosted glass cards with 1px semi-transparent borders and subtle neon glows on hover."
  • Give it constraints: "Ensure the padding is consistent (py-24) across all sections."
  • Visual Feedback: If something looks off, take a screenshot, drop it into the terminal, and ask "Why is this button misaligned?"
  • Mobile First: Always remind your engineer: "Make sure the grid collapses to 1 column on mobile (md:grid-cols-2)."
